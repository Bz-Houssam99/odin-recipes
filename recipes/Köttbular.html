Swedish Meatballs (Köttbullar)

Description

Köttbullar (Swedish meatballs) are delicious, tender meatballs, often served with mashed potatoes and lingonberry jam.

They’re a comforting, classic Swedish dish that’s both savory and slightly sweet. Often enjoyed as a hearty meal or snack, these small, golden-brown meatballs are both cozy and crowd-pleasing, embodying the essence of Swedish home cooking.

Ingredients

  • Butter or oil (for frying)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 500g ground beef
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Mix: In a bowl, combine the ground beef, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape: Roll the mixture into small meatballs.
  3. Fry: Heat butter or oil in a pan. Fry the meatballs until browned and cooked through (about 5-7 minutes).
