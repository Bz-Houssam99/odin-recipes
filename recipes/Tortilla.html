Spaghetti Tortilla

Description

Tortilla Espa√±ola is a delicious Spanish omelette, often enjoyed as a quick meal or snack. Its rich, comforting taste makes it a favorite in Spanish kitchens.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Fry the potatoes in olive oil until soft and tender.
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt.
  3. Mix the fried potatoes into the beaten eggs.
  4. Cook the mixture in a pan, shaping it into a thick omelette, and cook until the edges set.
  5. Flip the tortilla to cook the other side until golden and firm.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature.
