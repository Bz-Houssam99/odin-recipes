Spaghetti with Garlic and olive oil

Description

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a simple and flavorful Italian pasta dish, perfect for when you want something quick and satisfying.

It’s light, comforting, and full of delicious, aromatic flavors.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Parsely (optional)
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
  3. Add garlic to the pan and sauté until golden brown, being careful not to burn it.
  4. If you like some spice, add red pepper flakes.
  5. Once the pasta is done, reserve a little pasta water and then drain the rest.
  6. Toss the cooked pasta in the garlic oil, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if necessary to help coat the noodles.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley (optional) and serve immediately.
